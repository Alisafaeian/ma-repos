Ali Alipour

Iranian footballer Ali Alipour Ghara is an Iranian international footballer who plays as a forward for Gil Vicente. At the age of 19 years and 185 days, Alipour was the youngest-ever player to score in the Tehran derby after scoring in Persepolis' 1–0 victory on 15 May 2015. Wikipedia
